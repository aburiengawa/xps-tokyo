We provide 3 main ways to relocate:


FULL COVER

Usually larger house moves


U-PACK

For families of all sizes on a budget


MINI-MOVES

Singles/couples using a van





Full Cover


For busy people and families. As effortless as a move can be. This service takes the hassle out of relocating. All you have to do is tell us what you want and we do the rest. Each detail is important and our years of experience mean smooth sailing.


Packing and unpacking, arrangement of furniture all as per your instructions, you are free to do what only you can do. We can even supply help with the children on the day of the move.


Price considerations — Size, distance, accessibility, building requirements, unusual items


Insurance — Standard cover of up to ¥3,000,000. Additional cover available at competitive rates.


Quote given after survey



U-Pack


This service supplies you with an economical option but a minimum amount of work for you. The packing materials are delivered to you and you can pack (and pare) at your convenience. We also supply you with a sheet of tips and how-to's. No need to pack furniture and appliances as we do this as part of the move and done prior to loading.


After delivery, can unpack at your convenience and the packing materials are collected by as when ready. For very delicate items we suggest that we pack on moving day.


Price considerations — Size, distance, accessibility, building requirements


Insurance — Standard cover of up to ¥1,500,000. Additional cover available at competitive rates.


Quote given after survey



Mini moves


Fast and cheap. For singles/couples who haven't acquired a truck load of things. Usually done in a 1 ton van, things can be a small bed, small fridge, TV, washer, suitcases and boxes + you!


Price range — From ¥10,000 ~


Price considerations — Size, distance, accessibility (staircases).



Note: Inquire about any household items we may have. If you need something, have it delivered at the same time and make a saving!
